How to set up your wifi router?

First of all, we have to plug in your router to the wall socket. After that you can see in your router there are four lights. Check if it's blinking or not. If not press and hold the wifi button for 10 seconds till it start blinking. Then go to your laptop on the right-bottom you can see wifi icon. Click on it and enter password to connect.

How to reset the router?

First of all you have to check how many lights you have on router? If they are four lights which means your router is good but sometimes we are not able to connect due to configuration mismatch. So in that case just look in backside of router you can see hole on backside of router so you have put the paperpin or needle in the hole and press the button and hold it down for 30s. After 30s your router configuration will be reset. This is way we can reset the router.
